# 30-Day Expansion Plan (Post Sprint)

## Objective
Convert the 10-day sprint output into repeatable paid pilot execution and lane expansion.

## Week 1 (Days 1-7): Close + Baseline
- Convert top pilot prospects using terms sheet and close card
- Lock baseline metrics for each pilot account
- Ensure one named pilot owner per account

Success criteria:
- 1 signed paid pilot
- Baseline sheet completed for each active account

## Week 2 (Days 8-14): Execution + Reliability
- Run live workflows in production pilot context
- Track failures by taxonomy and patch quickly
- Capture first KPI snapshot and staff quote

Success criteria:
- Weekly KPI review completed
- First pilot proof worksheet filled

## Week 3 (Days 15-21): Proof Packaging
- Convert worksheet outputs into customer-facing mini case study
- Publish anonymized proof narrative
- Tighten objection handling from real pilot conversations

Success criteria:
- 1 finalized case study
- Updated close script based on field objections

## Week 4 (Days 22-30): Expand Lane Coverage
- Add second procedure lane for successful pilot account
- Extend to additional payer/Lob combinations
- Formalize expansion offer terms

Success criteria:
- At least one account expanded beyond initial lane
- Repeatable onboarding checklist validated

## KPI Targets (30-Day)
- Signed paid pilots: 1-2
- Active weekly users per pilot: >=1
- Case-study-ready accounts: >=1
- Expansion-ready accounts: >=1

## Risks + Mitigation
- Risk: low adoption despite pilot
  - Mitigation: narrow lane scope and weekly owner check-ins
- Risk: noisy KPI signals
  - Mitigation: enforce baseline capture and consistent windowing
- Risk: technical buyer delays
  - Mitigation: use security/procurement pack and traceability proof

## Owner Cadence
- Monday: pipeline + conversion review
- Wednesday: pilot execution review
- Friday: KPI proof + objection review + patch list
